Detailed Project Description

Background

The client sought a calm, daylight-optimized home with durable, low-maintenance finishes suitable for family living and frequent hosting. The design needed to balance contemporary aesthetics with functional living spaces, incorporating outdoor courtyards and open-plan interiors for natural light and airflow.

Design / Color Concept

We selected a warm neutral spectrum with a subtle contrast wall to anchor the courtyard views. Finishes emphasize texture: matte interior walls for softness and mineral coatings outdoors for longevity. Accent walls, trims, and custom detailing were carefully chosen to complement the natural light, creating a harmonious flow throughout the residence.

Execution Process

Surface preparation included priming, sanding, and micro-filling for seamless planes. Low-VOC paint systems were applied with airless spray in high-traffic zones, ensuring uniform coverage and faster turnaround. Special attention was given to delicate surfaces, such as wood trims and metal fixtures, with protective coatings to maintain durability.

Material Selection & Sustainability

All paints and finishes were selected with sustainability in mind. Low-VOC and eco-friendly products were prioritized to ensure healthy indoor air quality. Durable exterior coatings were applied to withstand Nairobi’s weather conditions, reducing long-term maintenance and enhancing the lifespan of surfaces.

Special Features

The project features customized wall textures, accent panels, and decorative finishes tailored to the client’s vision. Courtyard spaces were treated with weather-resistant paints, while interior areas used washable and easy-to-maintain coatings to ensure lasting elegance. Each space was designed to balance aesthetics, comfort, and functionality.

Outcome

The completed project showcases a seamless blend of modern design, natural light, and high-quality finishes. The residence exudes warmth, sophistication, and practical functionality, providing the family with a comfortable and stylish living environment. Caparol Paints’ coatings ensured consistent color, durability, and a visually appealing finish throughout the home.
